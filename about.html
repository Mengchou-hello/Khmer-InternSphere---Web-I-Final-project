About Khmer InternSphere

Bridging the gap between education and employment in Cambodia

Our Story

Khmer InternSphere was founded in 2022 by a group of passionate educators and industry professionals who saw a critical gap between what students learn in classrooms and what employers need in the workplace.

We started with a simple mission: connect talented Cambodian students with meaningful internship opportunities that build real-world skills and create pathways to successful careers.

Today, we've helped over 500 students find internships at 120+ leading companies across Cambodia, from Phnom Penh to Siem Reap and beyond.
